About the Exhibition

The Chemistry Exhibition 2025 celebrates innovation in science and showcases experiments exploring the frontiers of chemical discovery, sustainability, and technology. Visitors experience interactive displays, creative research, and real-world chemistry applications.

Exhibits

Chemistry In Space

Exploring chemical processes beyond Earth.

Overview: Investigate molecular formation in cosmic environments, from star birth clouds to planetary atmospheres.

Team: SpaceChem — guided by Dr. Sankalp.

Green Chemistry Revolution

Sustainable innovations in chemical processes.

Overview: Research on minimizing hazardous waste and improving renewable chemical manufacturing.

Team: EcoChem — mentored by Dr. Sankalp.

Forensic and Analytical Chemistry

Unveiling truth through chemical evidence.

Overview: Analytical tools used in crime investigation and environmental monitoring.

Team: ForensiChem — mentored by Dr. Sankalp.

Polymer Art and Smart Materials

Blending creativity with chemistry.

Overview: Smart polymers responding to light and temperature integrated into functional art forms.

Team: PolyArt — mentored by Dr. Sankalp.

Chemistry in Fireworks

Spectacular colors through reactions.

Overview: Metal ions and combustion create the breathtaking color displays of pyrotechnics.

Team: Spectra — mentored by Dr. Sankalp.

Exchange Programs

Students visited a marble factory observing crystal formation and later explored forensic laboratories in Spain and CERN, Switzerland, understanding applied analytical and nuclear chemistry in real contexts.
